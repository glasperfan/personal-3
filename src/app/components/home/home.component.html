About

I'm now living in the Northwest, and I keep busy with a million little projects. Statistically, there's a good chance one of them is a million-dollar project. You can see the results from some of them below.

I'm participating in as many duathlons, triathlons, and long-distance cycling events as I can possibly fit in. The BMC Time Machine SLR02 is a natural extension of my being. Goals for 2017 include an olympic triathlon and 2500 miles of cycling. You can check Strava to see how I'm doing.

I also spent 4 years at Harvard playing piano with the Hasty Pudding Theatricals, and I arranged and orchestrated starting as a sophmore. I continue to orchestrate extensively as an alumnus.

I continue to do freelance work both as a web developer and as pianist for almost any rehearsal or occasion. Please reach out if you'd like to hire me!
